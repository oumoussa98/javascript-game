Guess the number game

How to play ?

I have a random number in my mind and you have to find that number

when you try you'll see some letters, they gonna help you to find the number, here is what they mean

F - means that this integer doesn't match my random number
P - means that this integer matching my random number but in a diffrent position so you have to switch the his position
T - means that this integer is matching my random number and it's in the right position
